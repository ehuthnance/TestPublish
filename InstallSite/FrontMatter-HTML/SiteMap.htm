Site Map

The Site Map topic contains an outline of the User or Installation site. The entries correspond to the menu items on the left-hand menu pane. Only items at the lowest node level contain clickable links.

 

Tip:  To copy the entire content of this topic, first click anywhere around the topic title, on white space, being careful not to click on any links. Next, press Ctrl+A on the keyboard to select all content. Press Ctrl+C to copy.
To remove all formatting, paste into Notepad. To preserve menu indentation, paste into MS Word. In Word, press Ctrl+A (Select All), and then press Ctrl+Shift+F9 to remove the hyperlinks.
